Baidu apollo is a high performance, flexible architecture which accelerates the development, testing, and deployment of Autonomous Vehicles. And it is a open platform , we are continually updating.

My main work is to research and develop path planning algorithm for Apollo platform. In addition, I also researched on the algorithm that predict the trajectory according to the surrounding vehicles.
