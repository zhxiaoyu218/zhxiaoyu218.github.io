Baidu apollo is a high performance, flexible architecture which accelerates the development, testing, and deployment of Autonomous Vehicles. And it is a open platform , we are continually update the algorithm.

My main work is to research and develop path planning algorithm for Apollo. And I also researched on the algorithm that predict the trajectory surrounding vehicle.

The latest path planning algorithm, which is what I am working, is the Lattice planner in Frenet frame.

In Frenet Coordinates:

  • Frenet frame is such coordinate that represent the vehicle in two state, Longitudinal states s and Lateral states l.
